Methodology

Power Ratings

Base ratings derived from KenPom adjusted efficiency margins. Modified for:

  • Injury impact (manually assessed per player)
  • Travel distance to venue
  • Home court / crowd advantage
  • Coach tournament pedigree (Izzo, Self, Hurley)
  • Momentum (conference tournament performance)

Monte Carlo Simulation

10,000 full tournament simulations using logistic win probability model.

Each game's outcome is probabilistic based on the effective power rating difference.

Every ~1 point of rating difference = ~3% win probability shift.

Betting Model

Compares model-implied probabilities vs. historical seed-based upset rates.

Edges > 3% are flagged. Kelly Criterion used for sizing (5% base unit).

Will be updated daily with actual spreads/lines from DraftKings and Fanatics.
